shows going on: 
* COLIN MELOY (of the DECEMBERISTS) with Eleanor Friedberger at Durham's
Carolina Theater,

To WIN TICKETS for one of these shows, send an email to
mailer@catscradle.com with the subject line "Nov6" and rank the three
shows IN ORDER OF HOW MUCH YOU'D LIKE TO ATTEND EACH ONE, from MOST to

for each show, with winners notified Nov. 3.
